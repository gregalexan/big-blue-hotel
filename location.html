Amaliapolis

One of the most beautiful areas of Magnesia, in the historic Amaliapolis, embraced on one side by the azure waters of the western Pagasetic Gulf and on the other by the golden-green of endless olive groves, is the newly built, BIG BLUE HOTEL, which begins its operation, life, and activity in the heart of spring, in May 2022

You essentially have many possibilities, relaxing to make the most of your day. You can also enjoy sea games, enjoy coffee, drinks, refreshments, snacks, and ice cream at the nearby beach bars, on the sun loungers (provided free of charge, as well as showers) under the umbrellas and tall plane trees or at the surrounding cafes. You can also dine in traditional beautiful taverns with exquisite local appetizers, meats, cooked dishes, and seafood. Don't forget to try local cheeses, wines, and tsipouro. The operation of a mini market and bakery with all kinds of goodies can make your stay easier.

Tours

At a very close distance to the Big Blue Hotel in Amaliapolis, you'll find the town of Almyros (20km) with the renowned forest of Kouri, Anchialos, and the airport of Nea Anchialos, Volos, and Pelion, the famous mountain of the Centaurs. Amaliapolis (Mitzela) is approximately 280 kilometers from Athens and 230 kilometers from Thessaloniki.

Furthermore, you can explore the Sporades Islands with Sea Tours arriving in the port of Amaliapolis and staying within the budget.
